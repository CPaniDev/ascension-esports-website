About Coneglianum

CEO of the Ascension Esports organization, Coneglianum is one of the most well-known ADCs in the Italian competitive scene. With being Rank 1 on his signature pick, Ashe, for 8 seasons, people always tend to associate the champion with him. He's known for his team-centered playstyle, and he's a leader and a mentor for the rest of the team.
